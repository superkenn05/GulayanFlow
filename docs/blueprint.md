# **App Name**: GulayanFlow

## Core Features:

- Dashboard Overview: Provides an immediate overview of total products, highlights low-stock items, shows recent activity, and displays visual charts for inventory trends and usage.
- Product Management: Allows staff to add new products with details like name, category, image upload, price, and initial stock; and to view, edit, or delete existing products. Includes search and filter capabilities.
- Stock Tracking & History: Enables recording of all stock movements, including new inventory entries, items sold/transferred out, and waste/damaged items with reasons. Provides a searchable and filterable transaction history.
- Category Management: Tool to add, edit, or remove product categories (e.g., Vegetables, Fruits, Nuts), with support for assigning descriptive icons to each category.
- Low Stock Alerts: System highlights products with stock levels below a predefined threshold, offering clear visual warnings on the dashboard and product lists.
- Staff Profile: A basic profile page allowing staff to view their assigned name, role, and manage their login credentials.
- AI Inventory Insights: A generative AI tool that analyzes current inventory data and historical transactions to provide actionable market insights, identifying popular products and suggesting optimal stock levels to assist in purchasing decisions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant, fresh green (#248A37) inspired by the natural produce and growth, ensuring strong contrast on a light background.
- Background color: A subtle, desaturated green tint (#F4F7F5), providing a clean and refreshing canvas for content while maintaining consistency with the brand's natural aesthetic.
- Accent color: A brighter, analogous lime green (#8FE659) to draw attention to interactive elements and highlight important information without overpowering the primary color.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its clean lines and excellent readability across various screen sizes, consistent with a modern, user-friendly interface.
- Leverage Material-UI (MUI) icons for all interface elements, including product categories, actionable buttons (add, edit, delete), and visual representations of stock transactions, for a cohesive and recognizable iconography.
- Employ a mobile-first responsive design approach. Present product items in elegant card layouts for easy browsing on any device, and utilize clean, structured tables for displaying stock records and transaction history effectively.
- Implement smooth, subtle transitions and micro-animations throughout the application, especially when staff perform actions such as adding or editing products, updating stock quantities, or navigating between different sections of the system, enhancing the perceived responsiveness and user experience.
